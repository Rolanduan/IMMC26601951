\section{Variable Definitions}

\subsection{State Variables}

State variables describe the system's dynamic state:

\begin{itemize}
\item $\mathbf{v}_k(t)$: Position of vehicle $k$ at time $t$
\item $I_w(t)$: Inventory level at warehouse $w$
\item $\tau_{ij}(t)$: Travel time on road $(i,j)$
\item $D_i(t)$: Remaining demand at point $i$
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Decision Variables}

Decision variables are controlled by the optimizer:

\begin{align}
x_{ijk} &= \begin{cases} 
1, & \text{if vehicle } k \text{ travels } i \to j \
0, & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases} \label{eq:route_decision} \
y_{ik} &= \begin{cases}
1, & \text{if customer } i \text{ assigned to vehicle } k \
0, & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases} \label{eq:assignment}
\end{align}

\subsection{Parameters}

Fixed system parameters:

\begin{itemize}
\item $d_i$: Demand at customer $i$
\item $C_k$: Capacity of vehicle $k$
\item $[e_i, l_i]$: Time window for customer $i$
\item $\alpha = 0.85$: Reliability threshold
\end{itemize}
